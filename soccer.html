Soccer Memories

I started playing soccer when I was four years old, and my father has coached me for most of my life. His passion for the game is the reason I started playing soccer. Every weekend he and I love cheering for our favorite English Premier League team, Manchester United. Whether I’m playing competitively or just messing around with friends, I experience many benefits from this exciting sport: it is fun, keeps me active, and builds friendships with other teens. Throughout the years, I have played in various tournaments all over North Carolina and Virginia. My favorite locations are Boone, Virginia Beach, and Wilmington.

Football with Friends

During the offseason, at least once a week, you will find me at the fields kicking a ball around with friends. The camaraderie and competitive spirit on the field is unique and inspirational.
