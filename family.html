My Loving Family

My family is a family of five, and I have two sisters. My father is a project manager that helps integrate 5G onto cell towers. My mother is a stay-at-home mom, and she homeschools me and my younger sister. My older sister, Meredith, attends Wake Forest University and is pursuing a double major in computer science and math. My younger sister, Abby, loves reading, painting, and making crafts for everyone in the family. We all enjoy playing family games together. Some of our favorite games to play together are Quixx, Hearts, and Five Hundred. We also enjoy completing escape rooms and other cooperative games like Forbidden Island or Oregon Trail.
